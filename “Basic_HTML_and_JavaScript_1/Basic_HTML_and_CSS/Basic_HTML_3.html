The FIFA World Cup

...The FIFA World Cup is an international football competition held every four years, bringing together the best national teams from around the world.

History

The World Cup was first held in 1930 and has since become the most prestigious tournament in international football. It has a rich history, featuring memorable moments, legendary players, and unforgettable matches.

Tournament Format

The tournament features a qualifying phase, leading to the final competition with 32 teams. The teams compete in a series of matches, culminating in the crowning of the world champion.

Host Countries

Each World Cup is hosted by a different country, providing a global stage for nations to showcase their football talent and cultural richness. Host cities buzz with excitement as fans from around the world gather to support their teams.

Notable Moments

The World Cup has produced countless memorable moments, including stunning goals, dramatic upsets, and displays of extraordinary skill. These moments become etched in the history of football and are cherished by fans worldwide.
